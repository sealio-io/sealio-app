Entrepreneur

Masahiko Yamada

As the founder of the japanese company called Wacom. Masahiko Yamada paved the way for digital art in the tech industry. Back in the 1980s computers where see as just a piece of hardware for you to do work and have no creativity. But with the creation of the Wacom pens Masahiko proved otherwise. By creating a device that used a computer, which could in turn create artwork that was absolutetly amazing and shook the tech industry.
